Project Findings

1. The colorectal tumor and matched normal samples showed strong differential gene expression overall.

2. After filtering low-count genes, 31,516 genes were retained for analysis.

3. DESeq2 identified 13,043 genes with adjusted p-value less than 0.05.

4. The volcano plot showed many genes with both strong fold-change and strong statistical significance.

5. The heatmap of the top 30 genes showed clear clustering by condition, with normal and tumor samples separating into distinct expression patterns.

6. Several top genes were strongly upregulated in tumor samples, while others were strongly downregulated relative to normal tissue.

7. The project demonstrates that the dataset contains biologically meaningful signal and is suitable for bioinformatics analysis and visualization.
